I am a interdisciplinary artist/designer with a trade in fabrication and a focus on fine detailed illustrations.

A big influence to my work is being dyslexic, to which has forced me to adapt to a mostly monochrome world. As long as I can remember I have utilised color to understand concepts and remember content. It has given me a deep insight into the power color holds to convey meaning, emotion, and ideas. Having experience in hospitality from the age of thirteen, I am keen to how the smallest of details affect people’s experiences and moods. I am drawn to creating memorable and enjoyable experiences, and always focused and intentional with every detail I design. I am not scared at failing – I’m willing to take risk that other people are scared to try. Current passion project involves exploring and developing font and colours for the nurodivergent community.
